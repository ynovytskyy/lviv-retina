Dear colleagues!

I cordially invite you to take participation in the second conference "Retina Lviv 2018" (innovations and controversies in vitrеoretinal surgery).
The Сonference will be held May 4-5, 2018 in the city of Lviv, Ukraine.

Topics of the conference:

  • - Controversies in vitreoretinal surgery

  • - New surgical techniques, innovations

  • - Laser coagulation in retina diseases

  • - Complications of vitreoretinal surgery, retinal complications of anterior segment surgery

  • - Inferior retinal detachments. Causes and management of recurrences

  • - Vitreoretinal surgery for eye injury


Also in the program:

  • - Lectures

  • - Coffee with the experts

  • - Short questions and answers

  • - Presentation of complex cases

  • - Video session of Alcon

  • - Wetlab for vitreoretinal surgery

ABSTRACT SUBMISSION

Submission Deadline: 31 january 2018


TERMS AND CONDITIONS

  • All submissions must be made online by the deadline, 31 January 2018.
  • Submissions by post, email, fax or any other means will NOT be accepted.
  • Once you have submitted your abstract you will be sent an email summary. Check this to make sure all information is correct. The organizers will not accept any responsibility for abstracts that have not been submitted properly.
  • All abstracts will initially be reviewed, graded and accepted or rejected by the Programme Committee.
  • The Committee reserves the right to determine whether a free paper submission is accepted. The decision of the Committee is final.
  • If an abstract is accepted the named presenter must be a registered delegate at the Meeting and give the presentation.


ABSTRACT FORMAT

All submitted abstracts must be in Ukrainian or English and follow the structured format below:

  • Title, Setting, Purpose, Methods, Results, Conclusions, Financial Disclosure of all authors
  • Volume of abstract is max two pages
  • When using abbreviations for the first time, care should be taken to spell them out in full with the abbreviation in parentheses after the full word/s the first time it appears in the text.


COMPLEX CASES PRESENTATIONS

  • Only one case report per presentation is permitted
  • Authors must present their case on a maximum of 5 PowerPoint slides. Each accepted case will be allocated 6 minutes (3 minutes for presentation and 3 minutes discussion).
  • There will be an award for the best case presentation.


NUMBER OF SUBMISSIONS

  • Each author may submit a maximum of two Free Papers and one Complex cases presentation.
